Dance Off (2015)

Dance Off is a boutique dance studio based in Palo Alto, California that promises to teach dancers and non-dancers the power and therapy of dance movement. The company wanted a branding that resonates with their values and makes them distinguishable. The key words from initial meetings were— strong, approachable, passionate and depicting movement.

Design Solution

Dancers always perform in front of an audience or a mirror. The logo simply illustrates the connection that a dancer has with the audience. The audience reciprocates to the dancer by letting themselves flow and the dancer channels energy from the audience for her performance. Each letter shows a distinctive pose. The illustration of the flowy fabric shows movement and the colors evoke a sense of passion.


Interface Design

The website was designed keeping a contemporary look and feel in mind. A well laid out grid system helped me in keeping the layout clean. The client wanted to use photographs instead of illustrations for the website to feature their current company dancers.


Print Collateral

We used illustrations similar to the logo throughout the print collateral. The artwork and images involve flowy clothing and movements along with sharp shapes, building a cohesive look and feel in our branding.
