Brand Identity

Bedlam and Boss is a home furnishing brand run by a couple Lorna(the Boss) and Kay(the Bedlam). They wanted to incorporate mythical creatures into the design, instead of regular tables and chairs. The concept was to create quirky elements that would represent Lorna and Kay. They wanted to steer clear of corporate, instead the terms "Steam Punk", fantasy and edgy were used to describe the feel of the design.

Poster + Logo Design

Dance Off is a dance company in the Bay-area, which specializes in jazz dancing. The logo was created to mirror the concept of dance battles where contestants face off each other. The poster was for their annual dance performance and I wanted to create a dreamy, evocative feel in the design with the use of the floating dress and ballet slippers. This project was part of my school assignment.

Logo Design

Topdown tours is a luxury tour company based in San Francisco. The tours provided by them are custom-made and in a Mini-Cooper. I was inspired by the California License plate for the design of the logo. Nothing says custom-made like the license plate of a car!

Web Design for a Virtual Expo

Virtual Solutions Showcase is an initiative of Sustainable Silicon Valley to provide a platform for partner companies to showcase their solutions to some of the most pressing problems in our area. Adhered to the company colors and fonts as part of the design.
