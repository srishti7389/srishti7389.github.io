the work

Brand Identity

Bedlam and Boss are a custom home furnishing brand run by a couple Lorna (the Boss) and Kay (the Bedlam). They wanted to incorporate mythical creatures into the design, instead of regular tables and chairs. The concept was to create quirky elements that represent Lorna and Kay. They wanted to steer clear of corporate and use "Steam Punk", fantasy and edgy as the feel of the design.

Poster + Logo Design

Dance Off is a dance company based in the Bay-area, which specializes in jazz dancing. The logo was created to mirror they concept of dance battles with contestants facing off. The poster was for there annual dance performance and I wanted to create a dreamy, evocative feel in the design with the use of the floating dress and ballet slippers. This project was part of my school assignment.

Logo Design

Topdown tours is a luxury tour company based in San Francisco. The tours provided by them are custom-made and in a Mini-Cooper. I decided to incorporate the concept of using a license plate as the logo because it immediately sends across the message that the tours are private/custom-made. Nothing says custom like the license plate of a car!

Web Design for a Virtual Expo

Virtual Solutions Showcase is an initiative of Sustainable Silicon Valley to provide a platform for partner companies to showcase their solutions to some of the most pressing problems in our area. Adhered to the company colors and fonts as part of the design.
