Instawork

Website Redesign

Instawork is a marketplace for local restaurant jobs. Their current business sign up page was not receiving enough sign ups. The task was to come up with a layout and redesign that makes most businesses want to use the site for finding their employees.
Suggestions I made to Improve Sign Up rate:
  • In order to increase credibility we could use brand names upfront that are currently using the product.
  • Instead of overwhelming them with long forms to fill out,we should ask for just an email address initially.
  • Make a fun "3 step process" segment to highlight how simple the site would make their hiring process.
  • Testimonials are great, especially if they come from credible sources, but instead of showing people's faces we should show the restaurant/brand as that will be more recognizable.
  • Showing businesses how many candidates we have on our site, we could add a "tracker" that shows number of people hired on Instawork in the last few days/weeks.

Net Positive East Palo Alto

Infographics

The Net Positive East Palo Alto is a project of Sustainable Silicon Valley. As work on the project was underway there was need for more public awareness about why the project is vital. We chose infographics as a means for effective communication with the community and I was assigned the task of designing a series of them.
Data recovered by the organization showed large differences in East Palo Alto when compared to the rest of Silicon Valley. The factors showing most disparity were education, population and income; the cities that showed a stark contrast from East Palo Alto were Atherton, Palo Alto and Mountain View. This was the reason for selecting these cities and factors for our infographics.

Earth Day San Francisco

Illustration  ·  Print

The SF Earth Day team wanted to promote the Street Fair by showcasing Earth Day posters, banners and flyers across the city as well as social media.
Each year the Earth Day promotions involve multi-colored and cheerful posters and web-page designs.This year had to have the same tonality yet be refreshing and attractive. Simplistic Illustrations were a concept that had not been tried yet and they seemed like the ideal design voice this year.
