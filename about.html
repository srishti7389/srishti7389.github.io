Srishti Mittal—Designer/ Dancer/ Doer

I’m a designer living in Palo Alto and I work for CPP Inc—The Myers Briggs Company. When I’m not creating mockups, I teach Hip-Hop dance classes in the bay area. Click here to see one of my class videos.

I lived most of my life in the traditional city of Chennai, India. I’ve always been an admirer of expressive arts. I became a professional dance instructor at the age of 16. Chose my “main” career in computer science very early on and straight out of college started working for a major IT company in India. Two years into the job, I decided to quit and move to California with my husband. It’s when I realized I wanted to take a few steps back and follow my dream of getting paid for thinking and solving problems creatively. I went back to school and took on lots of passion projects to fight my way into this competitive industry. I worked as a visual design volunteer for an organization, which is improving environmental and resource conservation in Silicon Valley. I helped early stage startups bring their product vision into life and now I am working at CPP Inc., to help people better understand themselves through our personality assessments.
