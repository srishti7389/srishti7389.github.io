Srishti Mittal—Designer / Dancer / Doer
 

The Short Bio

I’m a user interface and experience designer living in Palo Alto. I strive to create experiences that not only help users but also acheive business goals. In my spare time I conduct dance classes & workshops, cook and practice yoga.

The Story

I lived most of my life in the traditional city of Chennai, India. I’ve always been an admirer of expressive arts. I became a professional dance instructor at the age of 16. Chose my “main” career in computer science very early on and straight out of college started working for a major IT company in India where, surprisingly, my awesome organization and public speaking skills helped me. Two years into the job, I decided to quit and move to California with my amazing husband. It’s when I realized I wanted to take a few steps back and follow my dream of getting paid for thinking and solving problems creatively. I went back to school and took on lots of passion projects to fight my way into this competitive industry. I worked as a visual design volunteer for an organization, which is improving environmental and resource conservation in Silicon Valley. I believe we are living in the most exciting and innovative times and want to make the best of it. Drop me a line if you want to chat, give me feedback, or dance together!
